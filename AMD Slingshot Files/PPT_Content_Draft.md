# PHANTOM: AMD Slingshot Submission Deck Content

## Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Headline**: PHANTOM
- **Sub-headline**: Automated Satellite-Based Infrastructure Audit for Accountability
- **Theme**: Open Innovation | AI for Social Good
- **Visual**: A futuristic, dark-themed interface showing a satellite scanning a mountainous forest region (Meghalaya).

## Slide 2: The Silent Crisis
- **Problem**: "Ghost Roads" & Phantom Projects.
- **Context**: Government funds allocated for remote infrastructure often vanish into incomplete or non-existent projects.
- **The Gap**: Physical auditing in regions like Meghalaya is dangerous, slow, and expensive.
- **Visual**: A high-contrast split screen: One side showing a "Completed" government report, the other showing a dense, roadless jungle from a satellite view.

## Slide 3: The Vision
- **Solution**: A decentralized, privacy-first infrastructure auditor.
- **Core Hook**: Bringing the analytical power of a space agency to every government auditor’s laptop using AMD Ryzen AI.
- **Impact**: Real-time verification of taxpayer money.

## Slide 4: The PHANTOM Pipeline
- **Step 1: Data Ingestion**: Automated scraping of PMGSY (Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana) records.
- **Step 2: Satellite Sync**: High-res Sentinel-2 imagery acquisition via STAC API.
- **Step 3: CV Audit**: U-Net based semantic segmentation on the NPU to detect physical built-roads.
- **Visual**: A clean flowchart showing data flowing from a "Website" icon -> "Satellite" icon -> "AMD NPU" chip icon -> "Map Dashboard".

## Slide 5: Powered by AMD Ryzen AI
- **Why NPU?**: Local inference for massive satellite tiles (1024x1024) without cloud costs.
- **Privacy First**: Sensitive government audit data never leaves the device.
- **Offline Capability**: Auditors in the field (Meghalaya) can verify projects even without internet connectivity.
- **Visual**: A cinematic 3D render of an AMD Ryzen processor glowing with "NEURAL" energy.

## Slide 6: The Dashboard (Demo)
- **Features**: Interactive Leaflet maps, Satellite/Dark toggles, and discrepancy tiers (RED/AMBER/GREEN).
- **The "Audit Trail"**: Clicking a project reveals the "Ghost vs. Reality" evidence.
- **Visual**: A high-res screenshot of our actual dashboard (screenshot from our verification).

## Slide 7: Evidence-Based Auditing
- **The "Ghost Line" (Dashed)**: The path the government *claimed* was built.
- **The "Built Highlight" (Neon Green)**: The actual road the AI found.
- **The Discrepancy**: If the green doesn't follow the white, it's a PHANTOM project.
- **Visual**: Close-up of a map where a dashed white line goes through trees, with NO neon green highlight.

## Slide 8: Scalability & Impact
- **Campus as Micro-City**: Scale down to monitor campus pavement and utility health.
- **State as Mega-City**: Scale up to audit the thousands of projects in North-East India.
- **Waste Reduction**: Direct alignment with "Sustainable AI" by cutting financial and material waste.

## Slide 9: Social Good & Integrity
- **Authenticity**: Integrated citation and data source trackers.
- **Accountability**: Provides NGOs and citizens with raw, verified data to demand answers.
- **Trust**: Bridging the gap between policy and reality.

## Slide 10: The Future
- **Expansion**: Moving beyond roads to Bridges, Dams, and School buildings.
- **Conclusion**: PHANTOM isn't just a tool; it's the new standard for digital governance.
